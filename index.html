Frequently Asked Questions

OSHA requires readily available, sanitary toilet facilities for all employees. The number of units depends on the crew size. For 20 or more employees, specific ratios apply.

Is hand sanitizer enough, or do I need a handwashing station?

For exposure to chemicals, paints, caustics, or in many food-service environments, OSHA requires soap and running water. A standalone handwashing station is the best way to ensure compliance in these cases.

What's included in your rental price?

Our portable toilet rental prices include the delivery, placement, and pickup of the unit, as well as the legal and safe disposal of all waste by our licensed technicians.

What makes your service "compliant"?

Our units are maintained to the highest standards of cleanliness. We understand the specific OSHA and health department regulations and we provide the right equipment and service schedule to ensure your site or event meets them.
